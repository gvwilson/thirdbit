## World

- Loghan: one of the Ninety Kingdoms, setting for both books
- Ghohraj: a prosperous river town on the Sochla River, downstream from the Blight
  - The Dog Market: Ghohraj's famous open-air market, named after Eddard the Dog
  - The dam: holds back things floating downriver from the Blight
  - The Old Hall: the town's witness hall for namings, weddings, and remembrances
  - The garrison house: local center of royal authority
- The Blight: the dumping ground for the Pilots' leftover and failed magic
  - Fast Lake: at the center; a year may pass per normal day; contains magic that works in reverse
  - Safe roads: built by the Pilots to carry things in; still exist, somewhat
- Chaghan: neighboring kingdom; politically hostile; chose Yangyal as consul to insult Loghan
- Garheim, Former Tongsa, Latter Tongsa, Barra Bantang, Anuvatik: other countries mentioned
- Stale: home for the cloudherds

## Outline

### Chapter 1

- Tornado attacks Noxy and other cloudherds
  - She lands at Ghohraj, reunites with Thokmay
  - Injured Cloak needs time to heal, so Noxy stays while Aft continues to Chaghan to pick up a passenger
- Tornado has struck three times, always targeting people who helped the garrison
  - Thokmay believes it came out of a bottle retrieved from the Blight
- Mope stirs up trouble baiting a chai seller who doesn't like the Gifted
  - Takes refuge in the prince's room

### Chapter 2

- Noxy meets Princess Ogmin, Dolly, and Mope at dinner
- Yangyal arrives as Chaghan consul: diplomatic immunity makes him untouchable
- Mope helps some raccoons, a bear, and a crow rescue an ungifted horse
- Thokmay is writing a report to his father when Eddard the Dog's ghost appears in his room
  - Leaves him shivering with premonition about the bridge, dam, and Blight

### Chapter 3

- Sergeant Inambu reports on her visit to Blight to Thokmay
  - He asks her to spy on Yangyal, but they agree to defer because of the spell's cost
- Noxy goes to the market with Ogmin and Dolly
  - They meet Rust and Mukh
  - Mukh gives Ogmin a glowing wooden ring
- Noxy spots a barge on the river piloted by two raccoons with no human crew

### Chapter 4

- Noxy takes Cloak up for a flight
  - Sees raccoons sailing a barge
  - Noxy turns back without seeing the raccoons sell the horse back into slavery
- Mob led by Bortje has formed outside the garrison house gates
  - Horse's owner complaining: "People over beasts!"
  - Noxy disperses the mob by raining on them
- The missing farmer from the opening is found dead
  - Funeral service that evening
- Afterward, Noxy senses something old and tired in the Blight

### Chapter 5

- Prince sees mob harassing raccoons for using a water pump
  - Wants to force them to get water from the stable
  - Sergeant Gyalpa tries to dissuade him, but the prince is angry
- Tornado returns
  - It kills a Blight smuggler who had escaped custody
  - Sergeant Inambu collapses after apparently turning it back from the town
- Mope arrives, having rescueed the raccoons from Bortja's mob
- Aft returns carrying Learned Shudarga, who announces she has come to surrender

### Chapter 6

- Thokmay puts Shudarga in detention in the guest suite of the garrison house
  - She has brought printed copies of all of Loghan's laws, and more are being smuggled in
- Noxy has a run-in with the same chai seller that Moep argued with
  - She then visits Aft, who says he's leaving and she can meet them in four days
  - And bumps into Yangyal on her way back to the garrison house
- Noxy takes Ogmin and Mope up for a ride
  - Some backstory about things from the Blight being caught by the bridge
  - Noxy senses an invisible extra passenger on Cloak
- That night, Ogmin's ring compels her to leave with the raccoons
  - Mukh and Rust are waiting, and kidnap her

### Chapter 7

- Confusion the next morning when the everyone realizes that Princess Ogmin is gone
  - A witness saw a bear hauling a wagon after moonfall
  - Inambu's spell appears to locate Ogmin at the Blight's edge
- Thokmay accuses Yangyal, who swears innocence on his own blood
- Mope questions Shudarga with Noxy looking on
  - Turns into a debate about Gifted rights
- Thokmay prepares to ride into the Blight
- Vignette: Ogmin is with Rust and the raccoons
  - The latter are uncomfortable keeping her
- Ends with Thokmay writing a difficult letter to his father

# Problems with the Cloudherd Stories

- Noxy is the one who makes the sacrifice in the end

- How do Noxy and Thokmay grow (character development)?
  - In Cloudherd:
    - Make Noxy's bossiness and feeling of inadequacy compared to her mother much clearer
    - Reveal Thokmay's second thoughts about becoming king (need to explain the race for the throne)
      - But don't do anything with those second thoughts (yet)
      - "If Dorbu believes it, I have to think about it" vs. "If Yangyal believes it, I probably shouldn't"
  - In Bear:
    - Noxy and Thokmay are both trying to figure out who they now are, separately and together
    - Shudarga: "You aren't puzzle pieces. You don't have to fit in. You can just *be*."

- What is the second act of Cloudherd?
  - Jigme's troops arrive and Jigme recognizes Thokmay
    - This has to happen before Noxy and Yangyal help Thokmay escape
    - So Yangyal has to delay Noxy's plan in order to catch a crow and send a message to Choghan
    - Is this how Noxy finds out he's a spy?
    - Kechen brings down the crow with her sling, finds the note
  - Yangyal helps Thokmay escape
  - Insists that they go to Choghan
  - Injures Pillow rather than letting them go to Loghan-in-Loghan
  - They are attacked in the forest (leads to Alva's fight with Signurd and Alva's death)

- How does Cloudherd end?
  - Why do they go back to Stale for the finale?
  - Jigme is going to use Stalers as hostages to get cloudherds to flood Loghan-in-Loghan
  - Dorbu dies stopping Jigme from doing this
    - Dorbu to Indy: "Give me back my sword"
    - Jigme dies as well
  - Call it a mutiny rather than a rebellion
  - Noxy says "not yet" to the forest (rather than "no")

- Yangyal is a better magician than he has let on, and a spy
  - His one "weakness" is keeping pet crows
    - Finds injured ones in the forest, cares for them, releases them
  - Later reveal that he's using magic to use them as messengers to Choghan

- Set up the return of the Pilots
  - Pilot's spirit trapped in the Blight, freed by Mukh
  - Noxy's sensitivity plays a part in the second act
  - She can tell something is happening, but she thinks it's Yangyal's doing
  - Thokmay: "If I agree with Yangyal on something I need to re-think my belief"

- What happens with Shudarga, Yangyal, and Bortje in Ghohraj? (second act)
  - Yangyal is shocked by Shudarga's appearance: she's a much greater threat than Loghan vs Choghan
  - Equally shocked that Thokmay isn't doing anything about her
  - Yangyal maneuvers Shudarga to align with the Gifted, and uses Bortje to attack her
    - Does this to stir up trouble *and* to discredit Shudarga by making her appear radical
  - Yangyal uses Thokmay's fondness for Kulbinder and Mope against him
    - Easy to convince Bortje that Thokmay is on the Gifted side
  - Thokmay helps Shudarga escape (he's picking a side) but when/how?
    - Relies on Noxy to do this

- Delay Ogmin's kidnapping to the third act
  - Journey into the Blight is a good climax for Thokmay
  - How to connect it to previous events? (foreshadowing plus motivation)

- Bear ends with Rust having a change of heart and saving the princess from Mukh
  - Mukh dies
  - The prince and princess are rescued by Noxy

- Right now in Cloudherd:
  -  Why does Thokmay have scars?
- The bothy has to figure into the story again
- Alva dies
  - Signurd challenges Noxy after Alva's death (so she's not there to help)
- Noxy gets an idea watching Rash stand up to the *tralpa*: what is it?
- Rind gives birth
- The adult cloudherds return
- Dorbu takes his sword back from Indy
  - Rebels against the rebellion because of Jigme's deal with Yangyal
- Rash and Sensy go to the midsummer fancy, and Noxy goes with Thokmay (sort of)
- Need to mention the Flying Mountain

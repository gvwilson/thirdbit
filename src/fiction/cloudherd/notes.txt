# The Cloudherd and the Tiger's Boy

## Aims

- The law should belong to everyone, but people can go too far in pursuit of a good cause
  - Dorbu and Jigme are on the right side, but Jigme's willing to do bad things
- People are small compared to their world, but can still harm it
  - The forest is worried because the Hett are waking up the last Pilot
  - Instead of the Anuat, have a community of Hett in the bow
- We can choose some of who we become, but not everything
  - Noxy declines the forest's offer, knowing that she'll regret it
  - (She makes a bargain with Signurd instead of fighting him)
  - Thokmay decides to withdraw from the race for the throne

## World

- Loghan: one of the Ninety Kingdoms, and the setting for the story
- Chaghan: neighboring kingdom (has had border skirmishes with Loghan)
- Stale Remainders: a cloudherd village in the mountain
- Duck Droppings: another cloudherd village nearby
- Churya Mountains: home to the cloudherd villages

## Characters

- Noxy (Probably Noxious) (f)
  - PoV protagonist
  - Smart but headstrong: always thinks she knows what's best for people
  - Rationalizes her mistakes and browbeats her friends into going along with her
  - Idolizes her dead father
  - Character development: realizes that she can be wrong sometimes and that mistakes have consequences
- Sensy (f)
  - Noxy's friend, the only girl of her own age in the village
  - Has always measured herself against Noxy and come up short in her own mind
  - Has a teenage crush on Rash
- Rash (m)
  - Noxy's friend, the only boy of similar age in the village
  - Cocky, has a teenage crush on Sensy
  - But wants to see the world
- Indy (Literally Indigestible) (f)
  - Reeve of Stale and Noxy's mother
  - A natural leader
- Granna Fee (f)
  - Indy's mother / Noxy's grandmother
  - The village schoolteacher
  - She flirts with Grappa Gas, but doesn't take it too seriously
- Grappa Gas (m)
  - Aft's father / Granna Fee's suitor
  - Brave, full of tall tales, one-legged, a bit of comic relief but fierce when angered
  - He raised Aft on his own
  - More serious about Granna Fee than she is about him
- Aft (Disgusting Aftertaste) (m)
  - Grappa Gas's son / Rind's husband
  - Big and strong, fiercely protective of his wife Rind and the baby that's on its way
  - Argues with his father about everything
- Rind (f)
  - Aft's wife, originally from Duck Droppings
  - Gives birth during the story
- Ulfmaerr (deceased) (m)
  - Noxy's father
  - Was a magician but died during a plague (used his magic to save people)
- Scholar-Candidate Yangyal (m)
  - Stale's tralpa (government representative)
  - Appears to be an incompetent magician
  - Turns out to be a Chaghan spy and the major antagonist of the series
- Thokmay (m)
  - A prince (member of the extended royal family, possible contender for the throne)
  - Identity is a secret: was placed in the regiment to learn common ways, couldn't escape
  - Character development: his time among the soldiers has made him uncomfortable about his privilege
- Kulbinder (m)
  - A Gifted tiger, Thokmay's fiercely loyal bodyguard
  - Thokmay pretends to be the tiger's boy
- Sergeant Dorbu (m)
  - A natural leader
  - A true believer in Shudarga's cause
- Captain Jigme (m)
  - Dorbu's commander, and another true believer
  - Willing to do evil for the cause
- Private Kechen (f)
  - Rebel soldier, good with a sling
  - No qualms at all
- Alva (f)
  - Leader of the local trolls and the current voice of the forest
  - Old, and wants to pass on the responsibility
- Signurd (m)
  - A troll who wants to be the forest's voice and doesn't like humans
- The Herd of Trees
  - A conscious forest that wants Noxy to be its voice in the world
- Miscellaneous other villagers and soldiers
  - Most of Stale's adults are away rounding up the clouds during the story

# The Cloudherd and the Tiger's Boy

## Outline

Each chapter is about 6000 words, so 15 chapters in total

### Arc

-   Act 1: setup
    -   Major: Noxy's decision about becoming the voice of the forest
    -   Major: Thokmay's struggle between believing in the rebel cause and being royal
    -   Major: Dorbu's zealotry leading him to do bad things in a good cause
    -   Minor: Noxy dealing with growing up (Sensy and Rash getting together)
    -   Minor: Grappa Gas and Aft having a rupture they may not be able to repair
    -   Minor: attraction between Dorbu and Indy short-circuited by his zealotry
-   Act 2: the turn
    -   Major: Yangyal's attempted escape and death
    -   Major: Noxy offered the forest's power (discussion with Alva, fight with Signurd)
    -   Major: Thokmay struggles with his sympathy for the rebel cause ("If Yangyal is a royalist, I'm not")
    -   Major: Dorbu's zealotry (gradually imposes more control of Stale)
    -   Minor: Noxy dealing with Sensy and Rash's relationship
    -   Minor: Grappa Gas and Aft: Gas "betrays" Aft when he wants to get Rind to safety before the baby is born
    -   Minor: Dorbu and Indy flirting (heightens Noxy's feelings about Sensy and Rash) but also arguing
-   Act 3: finale
    -   Major: Noxy almost accepts the forest's power to save Stale, but Thokmay talks her out of it
    -   Minor: Noxy comes to terms with her father's death
    -   Major: Thokmay's struggle is unresolved: he's going home, but isn't sure who he'll be there
    -   Major: Dorbu dies
    -   Minor: Noxy plays Snakes and Whistles with Rash and Sensy and the littles at the fancy
        (reconciliation)
    -   Minor: Aft tells Grappa Gas that he's always welcome to spend time with his granddaughter
        (steps toward reconciliation)

## World

-   Loghan: one of the Ninety Kingdoms, and the setting for the story
-   Chaghan: neighboring kingdom which is backing Shudarga's rebellion for its own reasons
-   Stale Remainders: a cloudherd village in the mountain
-   Duck Droppings: another cloudherd village nearby

## Major Characters

-   Noxy (Probably Noxious) (f)
    -   PoV protagonist
    -   Smart but headstrong: always thinks she knows what's best for people
    -   Rationalizes her mistakes and browbeats her friends into going along with her
    -   Idolizes her dead father
    -   Character development: realizes that she can be wrong sometimes and that mistakes have consequences
-   Sensy (f)
    -   Noxy's friend, the only girl of her own age in the village
    -   Has always measured herself against Noxy and come up short in her own mind
    -   Has a teenage crush on Rash
-   Rash (m)
    -   Noxy's friend, the only boy of similar age in the village
    -   Cocky, has a teenage crush on Sensy
-   Indy (Literally Indigestible) (f)
    -   Reeve of Stale and Noxy's mother
    -   A natural leader
-   Granna Fee (f)
    -   Indy's mother / Noxy's grandmother
    -   The village schoolteacher
    -   She is seeing Grappa Gas, but doesn't take it too seriously
-   Grappa Gas (m)
    -   Aft's father / Granna Fee's suitor
    -   Brave, full of tall tales, one-legged, a bit of comic relief but fierce when angered
    -   He raised Aft on his own
    -   More serious about Granna Fee than she is about him
-   Aft (Disgusting Aftertaste) (m)
    -   Grappa Gas's son / Rind's husband
    -   Big and strong, fiercely protective of his wife Rind and the baby that's on its way
    -   Argues with his father about everything
-   Rind (f)
    -   Aft's very pregnant wife
    -   Originally from Duck Droppings and would like to move back
-   Ulfmaerr (deceased) (m)
    -   Noxy's father
    -   Was a magician but died during a plague (used his magic to save people)
-   Scholar-Candidate Yangyal (m)
    -   Stale's *tralpa* (government representative) and a minor villain
    -   Petty antagonist
-   Thokmay (m)
    -   A prince (member of the extended royal family, possible contender for the throne)
    -   Identity is a secret: was placed in the regiment to learn common ways, couldn't escape
    -   Character development: his time among the soldiers has made him uncomfortable about his privilege
    -   Will be the protagonist of the second book
-   Kulbinder (m)
    -   A Gifted tiger, Thokmay's fiercely loyal bodyguard
    -   Thokmay pretends to be the tiger's boy
-   Sergeant Dorbu (m)
    -   A natural leader
    -   A rebel: a true believer in Shudarga's cause
    -   Initially seems like a good guy, but is willing to do evil for his cause
-   Captain Jigme (m)
    -   Dorbu's commander
    -   Fewer qualms about doing evil for the cause
-   Private Kechen (f)
    -   Rebel soldier, good with a sling
    -   No qualms at all
-   Alva (f)
    -   Leader of the local trolls (gifted chimpanzees)
-   Signurd (m)
    -   Another troll who doesn't want dealing with humans
-   The Herd of Trees
    -   A conscious forest that wants Noxy to be its voice in the world
-   Miscellaneous other villagers and soldiers
    -   Most of Stale's adults are away rounding up the clouds during the story

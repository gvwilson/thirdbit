Two hundred years from now, in a warming Antarctica, a teenage clone Iffy lives aboard a fishing boat with her foster parents in the small port of Halley. Haunted by nightmares of her family's death, she struggles to understand why blueprints for machines she has never seen keep appearing in her mind - a gift she fears is a sign of AI contamination. When a machinist named Johnson Wales arrives in town, Iffy becomes tangled in a web of intrigue spanning rival Antarctic settlements, the aftermath of humanity's war with artificial intelligence, and a secret underground base that is once again accessible thanks to melting ice.

Iffy flees into the wilderness pursued by Doctor Johel, who intends to use her to seize command of the AI. There, Iffy discovers that she can communicate not only with the machines humanity still fears, but also with an emerging hybrid consciousness shared by the creatures that are slowly rewilding the continent. After an encounter with the electronic ghost of a long-dead soldier, Iffy must choose between trying to restore the past or taking a chance on a new kind of future.

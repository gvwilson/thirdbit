Dr. Greg Wilson is a programmer, author, and educator based in Toronto. He co-founded Software Carpentry, which has taught software skills to tens of thousands of researchers worldwide, and has authored or edited over a dozen books, including "Beautiful Code", "The Architecture of Open Source Applications", "Teaching Tech Together", and "Software Design by Example". Greg is a Fellow of the Python Software Foundation and a recipient of ACM SIGSOFT's Influential Educator of the Year award.

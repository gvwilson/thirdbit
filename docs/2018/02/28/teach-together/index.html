Workshop on Organizing for Progressive Community Change

⇐ previous
Posted 2018-02-28
next ⇒

A growing number of people who work in tech want to be involved in progressive community change, but have never been part of grassroots initiatives before. We are therefore pleased to announce that Alejandra Bravo and Graham Mitchell will be running a one-day hands-on workshop on Saturday, April 7 in Toronto to teach people how to figure out what their community actually needs, how to rally and organize volunteers, and how to build relationships with local institutions and like-minded groups. For more information, and to apply to attend, please go to http://teachtogether.tech/ and please share this announcement with anyone who might be interested.

(And if you’re looking for other events that Saturday, check out http://girlsintechcon.com, a one-day conference for girls in grades 6-8 who are interested in tech.)

⇐ previous
Posted 2006-10-06
next ⇒

I am very pleased to announce the first release candidate for DrProject 1.1. We’re very grateful for David Scannell1 for dozens of improvements and bug fixes. DrProject is a fork of Trac with:

  • support for multiple projects in a single installation;
  • integrated mailing lists;
  • tagging and tag clouds;
  • a web-based administrative interface; and
  • a scripting API.

It is being used in several courses at the University of Toronto, and at Queen’s University; we hope you’ll give it a try.

  1. A talented, conscientious, and likeable programmer who is now looking for a full-time job. ↩

Carl Boettiger, Scott Chamberlain, Ted Hart, and Karthik Ram have just published a paper titled “Building Software, Building Community: Lessons from the rOpenSci Project”, in which they describe what they’ve learned by growing the rOpenSci project There are a lot of great ideas here that other groups could borrow, and of course you can keep up with their news by following their blog.

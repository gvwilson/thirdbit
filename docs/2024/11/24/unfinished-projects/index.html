What I’ve learned about myself:

  1. I have lots of ideas, most of which don’t make as much sense to other people as they do to me.
  2. I start things before I’ve thought them through.
  3. I have trouble finishing things (largely because of #2).
  4. I enjoy tinkering with small pieces of software much more than I enjoy contributing to large ones.
  5. I enjoy teaching and mentoring more than I enjoy managing.
  6. I run out of steam when I’m working on my own or don’t have a specific audience and deadline.

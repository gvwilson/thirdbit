I am planning to run a Teaching Tech Together workshop online in September 2024. If you would like to take part, please email me.

  • When: 12:30-16:00 Eastern time on Wednesday, September 18 and Thursday, September 19, 2024.
  • Where: Zoom (link will be shared with participants).
  • Content: these slides based on the book Teaching Tech Together.
  • Topics:
    • creating learner personas
    • why novices, competent practitioners, and experts need different lessons
    • managing cognitive load
    • formative and summative assessment
    • what motivates and demotivates learners
    • teaching as a performance art
  • Cost: pay what you can (e.g., the price of dinner for two wherever you call home)

100% of the money raised will go to Rainbow Railroad, a global nonprofit organization that helps at-risk LGTBQI+ people get to safety worldwide. Since its founding it has helped almost 10,000 LGBTQI+ individuals find safety through emergency relocation, crisis response, cash assistance, and other forms of aid.

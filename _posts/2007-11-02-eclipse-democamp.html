--- layout: post root: ../../.. title: "Eclipse DemoCamp" date: 2007-11-02 10:52:02 --- IBM is organizing an Eclipse-specific DemoCamp up in Markham on Thursday, November 22. You can sign up to attend by adding your name to the wiki [1], and of course they're looking for Eclipse-related demos. This is just one of a series of DemoCamp-like events being held around the world; others will be in Austin, Beijing, Budapest, Chicago, Glasgow, Karlsruhe, London, Munich, New York, Ottawa, Portland, Stuttgart, and Vancouver, and the list just keeps growing. [1] My name would be there, but I've forgotten my Eclipse Bugzilla password, and as near as I can tell, I have to enter it in order to request a new password...

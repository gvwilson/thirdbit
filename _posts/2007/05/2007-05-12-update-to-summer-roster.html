--- layout: post root: ../../.. title: "Update to Summer Roster" date: 2007-05-12 12:24:02 --- I'm pleased to announce that we're adding one more student to this summer's team. Tony Balkissoon just finished an engineering degree at U of T; he has been very active on campus in efforts to stop the genocide in Darfur, and is heading to Harvard Law School in September. Thanks in part to support from Prof. Ron Deibert of the Citizen Lab, Tony will spend the summer writing student-friendly introductions to legal issues associated with software and the internet, including privacy, property rights, accessibility, and reverse engineering. I'll blog his work here as it appears; we look forward to your comments on it.

--- title: "It's Not You, It's Me" date: 2008-10-31 08:14:24 --- After a lot of thought, I have regretfully decided to resign as a contributing editor with Doctor Dobb's Journal. The magazine is still great reading, and Jon Erickson and his crew have been a rare pleasure to work with, but I simply don't have the time to keep up any longer. It's been a lot of fun --- I'll miss it.

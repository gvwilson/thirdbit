--- title: "Beautiful Testing" date: 2008-12-18 13:16:37 --- I'm very pleased to pass on Adam Goucher's announcement that he and Tim Riley (of Mozilla) will be helming a sequel to Beautiful Code called Beautiful Testing. Proceeds will go to Nothing But Nets, which provides mosquito nets to malaria-infested regions in Africa. BT will be the third "sequel", joining Beautiful Architecture (due out any day now) and Beautiful Data (which is just getting rolling). Congratulations!

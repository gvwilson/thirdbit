--- layout: post title: "Ironic, Isn't It?" date: 2008-12-23 16:15:58 --- Via Simon Willison: Merb (the "other" Ruby-based third-generation web programming framework) is being merged into Ruby on Rails 3. What this means in practice is that users will be able to choose which ORM they want to use, which templating framework to write their pages in, etc. Put another way, it means that every Rails app will potentially contain something you're not familiar with. Ironically, we chose Django over TurboGears precisely to avoid this...

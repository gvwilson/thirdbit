--- title: "As Excited as a Bumble Bee in a Squash Patch" date: 2011-08-19 01:08:45 --- Two weeks ago Hot Yam! (a sustainable food co-op at the University of Toronto) submitted a proposal to the Graduate Student Union to operate a full-time, student-organised cafe and student space in their building. Today, they heard that it has been accepted—congratulations! But as Jon Pipitone says in his blog post, it's going to take a lot of work to get off the ground, and they could use your help. You can reach them at hottestyam@gmail.com, or come by the GSU (16 Bancroft Avenue) at 6:00 pm on Monday, August 22 for a quick meeting about who, what, when, and how to make the world just a little bit better.

--- layout: post root: ../../.. title: "Your Brain Thinks Tools Are Part of Your Body" date: 2009-06-29 08:55:23 --- Interesting new paper titled "Tool-use induces morphological updating of the body schema", summarized in this Discover blog post: "using a tool for even a brief amount of time caused volunteers to update their body schema, and to consider the tool a part of their bodies." The effect is subtle, but measurable. Question: how many software tools have this effect?

--- title: "Sometimes You Can't Even Manage What You *Can* Measure" date: 2009-10-21 08:35:33 --- Via the CBC: Toronto will miss its target for reducing landfill waste because only 15% (!!) of apartment and condo buildings have green bins. 15% sounds pitiful, but one reason the figure is so low is that the drop in city revenues over the last 12 months has meant cutbacks in the green bin program. I wonder if this would be moving ahead faster if the city's web site had a dashboard that included week-by-week stats?

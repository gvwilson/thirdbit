--- layout: post title: "Survey: Theory vs. Practice" date: 2009-03-19 20:24:33 --- A couple of profs (one in Sweden, the other in New Zealand) have put together a short survey to find out if programmers write code the way they've been told is The Way, or do they ignore the theory and follow practices learned in the school of hard knocks. It only takes 10 minutes, and we all might learn something.

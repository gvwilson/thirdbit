--- layout: post title: "Google Summer of Code Timeline" date: 2009-03-01 06:49:34 --- The GSoC 2009 schedule looks like this:
  • March 9-13: organizations sign up
  • March 18: organization list published; student discussion starts
  • March 23-April 3: student applications
  • April 20: acceptance notification
  • May 23-August 10: talk, code, review, repeat
  • August 24: final evaluation deadline
Once again, it's very (very) late for Canadian students: April 20 is two weeks after the end of classes at U of T :-(.

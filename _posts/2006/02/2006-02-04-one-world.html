--- layout: post title: "One World" date: 2006-02-04 09:18:24 ---

As many have observed, Flickr's interesting photos page is addictive and beautiful. It's also inspiring, when you realize that there's no difference between photos from Thailand and photos from Thunder Bay.

And of course, the same 'net that brings us pictures from around the world also lets us listen in on Rebecca Wirfs-Brock's occasional musings. If she isn't in your blog roll, she should be; I just wish that her IEEE Computer articles were appearing there, so that the post-print generation could appreciate them too.

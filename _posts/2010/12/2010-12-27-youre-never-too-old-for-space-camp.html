--- title: "You're Never Too Old for Space Camp" date: 2010-12-27 22:11:03 --- I can't remember how I stumbled across BuyThisSatellite.org, but I'm glad I did. I've been a science fiction fan for (literally) as long as I can remember, and while I doubt I'll ever make orbit myself, there's something gosh-darned cool about actually buying a chunk of an actual orbiting satellite. Their web site says it best:
  • The owner of the world's most capable communication satellite just went bankrupt.
  • We're fundraising to buy it.
  • So we can move it.
  • To connect millions of people who will turn access into opportunity.

--- layout: post title: "An Interesting Comment on Academic Life" date: 2010-07-16 16:13:29 --- Beryl Lieff Benderly: "The Real Science Gap". The teaser says, "It's not insufficient schooling or a shortage of scientists. It's a lack of job opportunities. Americans need the reasonable hope that spending their youth preparing to do science will provide a satisfactory career." And later: "Because of long-ignored internal contradictions, however, the American research enterprise has become so severely dysfunctional that it actively prevents the great majority of the young Americans aspiring to do research from realizing their dreams."

--- layout: post root: ../../.. title: "Readability" date: 2009-03-10 09:04:33 --- Via Jon Udell (as so often): Arc90's new Readability tool is a decluttering widget for browsers that hides screen junk to make pages more readable. Which prompts a new design rule: every good web page should look the same before and after decluttering, just as every well-written program should look the same before and after application of a pretty-printer.
